The Importance of ISO 9001 and CE Certification in Kitchen Equipment

July 22, 2026

Certification is proof that a piece of equipment complies with certain quality and safety standards not just on paper, but throughout the manufacturing process.

What Does ISO 9001 Mean?

ISO 9001 documents that a manufacturer's quality management system complies with international standards. This means the production process is consistent and controlled.

The Importance of the CE Mark

The CE mark shows that a product complies with European safety, health and environmental protection standards. CE certification is nearly mandatory for businesses exporting abroad.

The Risks of Uncertified Equipment

Uncertified or low-quality equipment may look cheaper in the short term, but it increases the likelihood of frequent breakdowns, safety risks and problems during legal inspections.

Pre-Purchase Checklist

When buying equipment, request CE and ISO 9001 documents from the manufacturer, and always verify the warranty period and whether the certificates are up to date.
