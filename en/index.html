OUR PROCESS

How Do We Work?

We take your restaurant from dream to reality in four steps.

1. Free Inspection

We examine your premises on-site. We analyze your needs, take measurements and share initial ideas. This stage is completely free.

2. Planning & Design

3D modeling, kitchen layout, ergonomics planning and cost analysis. Our architects and engineers prepare the detailed project.

3. Production & Implementation

Custom equipment production, renovation works, electrical and plumbing, sign installation. We coordinate and follow all processes.

4. Delivery & Support

Equipment testing, staff training, warranty documents and 24/7 technical support line. We provide logistics support for international deliveries.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Curious About

Frequently asked questions about industrial kitchen and restaurant setup.

What are the prices of industrial kitchen equipment?

Prices vary depending on the project and equipment type. We have price lists for standard products such as stainless steel stoves, ovens and hoods. For custom production projects, we offer a free inspection service with a clear price quote.

How long does restaurant setup take?

It varies between 2-8 weeks depending on the size of the project. Small kitchen arrangements are completed in 1-2 weeks, while complete restaurant setup takes an average of 4-6 weeks. A detailed project timeline is provided during the planning phase.

Do you ship kitchen equipment abroad?

Yes, we offer safe and fast logistics solutions to over 50 countries. We complete your international projects seamlessly with our customs procedures, packaging and door-to-door delivery services.

Can I have custom kitchen equipment manufactured?

Absolutely. We manufacture kitchen equipment in custom sizes, designs and functions on stainless steel. We bring your desired product to life with 3D modeling and prototype production.

Do you have warranty and technical support services?

We offer a 2-year warranty on all our equipment. We are with you with our 24/7 technical support line, maintenance, repair and spare parts services after installation. Remote support is also available.
